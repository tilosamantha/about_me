Movies

Before I switched majors, I wanted to be in the film industry for a long time, first in animation and then my scope of interest got wider. I've always really liked movies since I was young. I love going to the theater and experiencing a movie when it's being released, and I'm lucky enough to live in a place like Utah that puts on a major film festival like Sundance every year.
On this page I'll share some of my favorite movies and releases I'm looking forward to.
