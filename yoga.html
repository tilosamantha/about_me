My love/hate relationship with Yoga
Yoga has never been one of my main choices of exercise. I find it incredibly frustrating and couldn't ever seem to find an appreciation for it. Back in December of 2019 for whatever reason I made it my goal to get into yoga. I wanted to be able to see what was so great about it.
I'm still not very strict with practicing yoga. I have some weeks where I am more commited than others. Recently I've recruited some friends to help keep me motivated and it works... sometimes. But I think I can safely say that I've made my peace with it now. It provides me with a mental break from work and studies, it's helped ease back pain for me and I feel good doing it. Yoga isn't ever an activity that I'd say is my favorite part of my day, but practicing it definitely has a positive influence in other aspects of my life.
